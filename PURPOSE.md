# Purpose of Spolia

## Core Mission

Spolia is a **hyperlocal liquidity engine** for the construction industry — a private, exclusive exchange that turns construction waste into a valuable, tradeable resource. By capturing the latent value in surplus and reclaimed materials, Spolia simultaneously reduces project costs and carbon footprints.

> "Every demolition site is a warehouse. Every renovation generates supply. Spolia connects that supply to demand."

Spolia is **not** a scrapyard. It is a premium, verified marketplace where quality is guaranteed through the **Spolia Bond** system, discovery is powered by the **Radar** feed, and impact is quantified on the **Impact Dashboard**.

---

## Target Audience

Access to Spolia is **verified and exclusive**. The platform serves:

| Role | Verification Requirement |
|---|---|
| **Architects** | Council of Architecture (COA) registration number |
| **Interior Designers** | Professional body membership or portfolio |
| **Contractors & Builders** | GSTIN / trade license |
| **Material Vendors** | Business registration + inventory proof |

All users undergo a manual or automated verification step before they can list or transact on the platform.

---

## Key Mechanisms

### 1. The Radar (Discovery Feed)
A real-time, geotagged feed of available surplus materials sorted by proximity. The Radar enables buyers to discover listings within their project radius, filtered by material type (marble, steel, wood, brick, glass, etc.), quantity, and price per unit.

### 2. AI Scanner (Beta)
A mobile-first camera interface powered by **Google Gemini Vision API**. Point the camera at a material stock — the AI identifies the material type, estimates volume and quantity, and suggests a fair market price. This dramatically reduces the friction of creating a new listing.

### 3. The Spolia Bond
A quality-guarantee mechanism. When a buyer commits to a purchase, a **refundable deposit** is locked in escrow. The deposit is released to the seller upon verified pickup. If the material quality does not match the listing description, the buyer can raise a **Quality Issue Report**, triggering a review. Sellers who repeatedly fail quality checks lose listing privileges.

### 4. Impact Dashboard
A data visualization layer that tracks the platform's collective environmental contribution. Metrics include:
- Total CO₂ emissions avoided (in tonnes)
- Total material weight rescued from landfill (kg)
- Per-user contribution breakdown
- City-level and platform-wide leaderboards

---

## Design Principles

1. **Exclusivity over volume** — a curated, high-trust marketplace, not a classifieds board.
2. **Hyperlocal first** — materials are heavy; distance matters. Listings are sorted by proximity.
3. **Zero-friction listing** — the AI Scanner makes it trivially easy to list materials correctly.
4. **Accountability through the Bond** — financial stakes ensure quality is not misrepresented.
5. **Impact as identity** — users see and share their environmental contribution as a badge of professional pride.
